## Notes

- gorilla/websocket single-writer contract: each conn has exactly one goroutine writing to it (the `writerLoop`); `ServeWS` reads from the conn in the calling goroutine.
- `SubscriptionManager.notify` is called from `FilterManager.fanOut` which holds `fm.mu` — keep `notify` lock-free (copy out sub slice under `subMgr.mu` quickly, then send to channels outside).
- Drop policy on full channel: skip silently (subscriber is too slow; they can re-query via `eth_getLogs`).
